Our Mission

Connecting People,
Horses & Healing

Kinship Equine Therapy exists to provide a safe, nurturing space where individuals facing emotional, behavioral, and developmental challenges can experience the profound healing that comes from authentic connection with horses.

Presence over PerformanceWe meet every participant where they are — with no agenda, only compassionate attention.
Safety First, AlwaysOur certified therapists and horses ensure a protected environment for every session.
Inclusive CommunityWe serve veterans, youth, trauma survivors, and families — regardless of ability or background.
Our Programs

Where Healing Begins

Each program is thoughtfully designed around the natural wisdom of horses — gentle guides on the path to wholeness.

🌿
Youth & Families

Building confidence, emotional regulation, and trust in young people through guided interactions with our gentle therapy horses.

Ages 5 and up  ·  Weekly sessions
🎖️
Veterans & First Responders

A safe space for those who carry invisible wounds. Equine-assisted work addresses PTSD, anxiety, and the challenges of transition.

No cost to veterans  ·  Certified EAGALA
🤍
Individual & Group Therapy

Therapeutic sessions led by licensed mental health professionals, using horse-assisted activities to explore emotions and foster growth.

All ages  ·  Insurance accepted
